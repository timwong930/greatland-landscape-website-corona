## Public business snapshot
- **Company:** Greatland Landscape, Inc.

- **Category:** Landscaping

- **Social:** https://www.facebook.com/greatlandlandscape/
- **Social:** https://www.instagram.com/greatlandlandscapeinc.8088/?hl=en

## What they do
A landscape maintenance company focused on HOA, apartment, townhome, and commercial properties in Riverside County and the Inland Empire.

## Why this looks like a good opportunity
- The site is template-heavy and missing a visible H1 structure on the homepage.
- It has social links, but the website itself could be reorganized into clearer service and request-for-proposal pages.
- There is a strong property-management niche here, which is ideal for a more focused lead-gen site and case-study layout.

## Suggested outreach / redesign angle
Position the redesign around HOA/property-manager leads: RFQ flow, service pages, and a cleaner proof-of-work structure.

## Starter site included in this repo
This repo includes a simple, responsive starter site in `index.html` and `styles.css` built only from public information.

### Draft structure
- **Maintenance plans** — Routine landscape care for multi-unit and commercial properties.
- **Irrigation and plant care** — Water-smart upkeep and replacements.
- **Property improvements** — Seasonal upgrades, cleanups, and replanting.
- **Request a proposal** — A simple RFQ form for property managers.
